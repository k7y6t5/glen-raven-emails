We believe textiles are essential ingredients in the design of extraordinary healing spaces. That’s why Sunbrella Contract with Defiance® fabrics are engineered with anti-microbial properties to meet the industry’s toughest standards in settings with the most strict performance and hygiene requirements.

It’s not enough for textiles to look beautiful; they have to perform, too. Designed specifically for high-traffic environments, Sunbrella Contract with Defiance fabrics offer sophisticated patterns, beautiful color palettes, soft textures and the ultimate in anti-microbial properties and cleanability.

Solution-dyed to repel liquids and stains, these fabrics have been engineered to offer exceptional UV performance and withstand rigorous bleaching without additional finishes or processing. Most fabric selections are independently certified to meet NSF/ANSI 336 Silver Level and UL-certified to meet GREENGUARD Gold Standards and will be promoted by the third-party Facts Certification program.
